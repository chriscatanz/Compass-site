Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Directlink ("we," "our," or "us") operates Compass, an AI-powered knowledge assistant for enterprise organizations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account registration information (name, email address, organization name)
  • Documents and files you upload to the Compass platform
  • Queries and conversations conducted with the AI assistant
  • Usage data and interaction logs for service improvement

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Compass service
  • Process and respond to your AI queries
  • Send administrative communications and service updates
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. Data Storage and Security

Compass runs on a private cloud architecture. Your documents and data are stored in an isolated environment and are never shared with third-party AI providers or used to train external models. We implement encryption at rest and in transit, role-based access controls, and comprehensive audit logging.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon termination, your data will be deleted within 90 days unless longer retention is required by law or contract.

6. Third-Party Services

Compass does not share your organizational data with third-party AI services. Infrastructure and support providers are contractually obligated to maintain confidentiality and security of your data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@directlink.ai to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time and will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy with a new date.

9. Contact Us

Questions? Contact us at privacy@directlink.ai or write to Directlink, Liverpool, NY 13090.
