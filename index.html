How do I reset a user's account access?
To reset a user's account access, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to Admin Portal → User Management
2. Search for the user by name or email
3. Click "Reset Access" and select reset type
4. User will receive a secure reset link via email
Source: IT Security Procedures v3.2

What's the escalation path for a fraud incident?
For suspected fraud, escalate immediately:

Level 1: Notify your direct supervisor
Level 2: Contact Fraud Prevention Team (ext. 4455)
Level 3: If over $10K, alert Compliance Officer

Document everything in the Incident Report System before end of shift.
Source: Fraud Response Protocol 2024

All new employees must complete within 30 days:

✓ Security Awareness Training (2 hrs)
✓ Compliance Fundamentals (3 hrs)
✓ Systems & Tools Onboarding (4 hrs)
✓ Department-specific certification

Track progress in the Learning Portal under "My Training."

How should I handle a customer complaint?
Follow the LEARN framework:

Listen actively without interrupting
Empathize with their situation
Apologize sincerely for the inconvenience
Resolve the issue or escalate appropriately
Notify supervisor if resolution exceeds authority
Source: Customer Service Guidelines

What's our policy on third-party data sharing?
Third-party data sharing requires:

• Signed Data Processing Agreement (DPA)
• Legal review for contracts over $50K
• Privacy Impact Assessment completion
• Approval from Data Protection Officer

No PII may be shared without explicit consent documentation.
Source: Data Governance Policy v2.1

Understanding Token Pricing

What is a token?

A token is a small chunk of text that the AI reads and writes. On average, a token is 3-5 letters or characters, so one word is usually 1-2 tokens.

FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can we get started?

Most institutions are live within an hour. Upload your documents, set permissions, and you're ready to go.

Where does our data go?

Nowhere. Compass runs in a private cloud infrastructure. Your data never leaves your control or touches third-party AI services.

How does Compass prevent hallucinations?

Every answer is grounded in your uploaded documents with source citations. If the answer isn't in your content, Compass says so.

What file formats are supported?

PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and plain text. We're adding more formats based on customer feedback.

Is there per-seat pricing?

No. We believe everyone in your organization should have access without licensing headaches. Simple, predictable pricing.

Can we control who sees what?

Yes. Role-based access controls let you define which teams or individuals can access specific document sets.
