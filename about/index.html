Our Story

Directlink started with a simple observation: organizations invest heavily in policies, procedures, and training materials, but that knowledge ends up scattered across shared drives, intranets, and filing cabinets.

Employees spend hours hunting for answers. New hires take months to ramp up. Compliance teams worry about inconsistent information. We knew AI could solve this, but most AI tools weren't built for enterprises with real governance requirements.

So we built Compass: a knowledge assistant that's private by design, cites its sources, and runs entirely in your infrastructure.
